Avocado Oil


Studies suggest that avocado essential oils can promote hair growth.
Avocado oil is commonly used as a carrier oil for essential oils.

Way of use:
Mix an ounce of avocado oil with three to six drops of peppermint or lavender essential oil for a homemade hair treatment.
You can also massage a nickel-size amount of avocado oil onto your scalp.

Avocado oil will help stimulate blood flow and unclog blocked follicles. These effects help fight hair loss.
